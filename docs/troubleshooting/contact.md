# Contact

Get in touch with the Synstream team for support, questions, or feedback.

## Support

### Email Support
For technical support, questions, or issues, please contact us at:
- **Email:** [contact.us@rtsinv.com](mailto:contact.us@rtsinv.com)
- **Response Time:** We aim to respond within 24 hours during business days

### Support Hours
- **Business Days:** Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (UTC+8)
- **Emergency Support:** Available for Enterprise customers

## Feedback

### Feature Requests
Have an idea for a new feature? We'd love to hear from you:
- **Email:** [contact.us@rtsinv.com](mailto:contact.us@rtsinv.com)
- **Subject:** Feature Request
- **GitHub Issues:** [Create an issue](https://github.com/RootsInnovation/synstream-docs/issues)

### Bug Reports
Found a bug? Please report it:
- **Email:** [contact.us@rtsinv.com](mailto:contact.us@rtsinv.com)
- **Subject:** Bug Report
- **GitHub Issues:** [Create an issue](https://github.com/RootsInnovation/synstream-docs/issues)

Include as much information as possible:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Expected behavior
- Actual behavior
- Environment details
- Logs or error messages

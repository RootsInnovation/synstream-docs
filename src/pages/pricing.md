# Pricing

## Choose the Right Plan for Your Team

Synstream offers flexible pricing plans to suit teams of all sizes, from small startups to large enterprises.

## Plans

### Free
- **Perfect for:** Small projects and testing
- **Features:**
  - Up to 10 pipelines
  - Basic connectors
  - Community support
  - 1,000 executions per month

### Starter
- **Perfect for:** Small teams
- **Features:**
  - Up to 50 pipelines
  - All connectors
  - Email support
  - 10,000 executions per month
  - Git integration

### Professional
- **Perfect for:** Growing teams and production workloads
- **Features:**
  - Unlimited pipelines
  - All connectors
  - Priority support
  - 100,000 executions per month
  - Advanced Git integration
  - Custom connectors
  - SLA guarantees

### Enterprise
- **Perfect for:** Large organizations
- **Features:**
  - Everything in Professional
  - Dedicated support
  - Custom SLA
  - On-premises deployment
  - Custom integrations
  - Training and consulting
